About Millie Parfait

Millie frequently displays traits that make her fans adore her, but she can also be harsh when she responds to her debut-mates and fellow Livers with profane remarks and retorts. She jumps on anything that captures her eye without considering if it is a smart idea, which frequently results in tumultuous talks with her.

On 18 June 2021, NIJISANJI EN opened two sets of auditions, one of which was for an all-female wave of four.[2] Silhouettes of the new Livers' designs were shown, one of which was designated "Cat of Darkness." The all-female auditions lasted until 11 July. YouTube channels for NIJISANJI EN's third wave were created on 14 September 2021, Their Twitter accounts were also created in September 2021.
